Welcome.

I'm currently a graduate student working with Dr. Nicole Landi in the Developmental division of the Department of Psychological Sciences at the University of Connecticut.

Broadly, my research centers around how the brain understands language. My dissertation focuses on language and category learning influence each other.

I am an Associate of the Neurobiology of Language program at UCONN. I also was a 2016 CT Institute for the Brain and Cognitive Sciences (IBACS) Summer Graduate Fellow and a 2017-2018 IBACS Research Assistant in Neuroimaging.

I also enjoy playing with data. Check out some of the sidebar links for some of the projects I've worked on.

In my spare time, I am passionate about cooking (check out my meal-prep challenges), comedy, and my adorable cat.
